San Francisco opens for business; masks off, capacity at 100 percent

After 15 months of requiring businesses to close or offer limited service, San Francisco — surpassing other major U.S.
cities in Covid shots with 70 percent of eligible residents fully vaccinated — will reopen today with businesses at 100
percent capacity and vaccinated patrons going without masks.
